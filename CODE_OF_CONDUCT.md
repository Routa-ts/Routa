# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Routa a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and tradeoffs.
- Help keep issues and discussions useful for maintainers and users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Insults, slurs, or discriminatory language.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, restrict participation, or ban contributors when behavior harms the project or community.

Report conduct concerns to `conduct@routa.dev`.

This code of conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant spirit for Routa's current project size.
